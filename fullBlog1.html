  • Is TCS calling employees to work from the office for five days?

       Last updated on December, 2022

    TCS asking its employees to come to office but its depends on project to project some where once a week and in another 2 days a week. Nothing is clear as 5 days a week.Yes they do have plan to ask employees to work mostly from Office where ever possible. want to run full fledged office for sometime before introducing new WFH policy which is getting ready parallelly.

    Another scenario is TCS offices capacity is around 4.8–5 lac employees where as today strength is more than 6 lakhs so can’t imagine where rest ~1.2 lakh will sit and work. this is across location so every location need to extended which will not happen in months. Another Scenarios people in few city’s like Delhi NCR, have more demands of seats than compare to other cities Bhuvneshwar, Kolkata, Banaras etc so it is going to be a problem again.

    So try to understand the problem Work from home will be there but need to follow instruction of management for working from office, go once or twice a week observe and present the issues you face - hopefully further going forward you will get work from home.
